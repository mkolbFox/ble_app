Privacy Policy - BLE Console

Effective Date: January 28, 2025

Overview

BLE Console is a Bluetooth Low Energy console application that prioritizes user privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bluetooth device names discovered during scanning
  • Macro button configurations you create
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device.

Bluetooth Permissions

BLE Console requires Bluetooth permissions to:

  • Scan for nearby Bluetooth Low Energy devices
  • Connect to and communicate with BLE devices
  • Send and receive data through BLE connections

No Internet Connection Required

BLE Console works completely offline and does not require internet connectivity. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from children or any users.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
